Notation Studio Guide

1. Adding Compositions

Click any cell in the grid to select it. Once selected, you can:
- Type directly using your keyboard.
- Click items in the left Palette to insert them automatically.
- Use the Template dropdown in the toolbar to load a sample composition instantly.

2. Formatting (Vocal & Tabla)

Select text inside a cell and use the top toolbar formatting buttons to apply standard Indian Classical notation marks:
- Mandra (M) / Taar (T): Dots below or above the note.
- Komal (K) / Tivra (T): Underlines or vertical lines.
- Double (D): Group notes together with a bottom curve (crescent).

3. Playback

Click PLAY to hear a synthesized playback of your notation. It reads exactly what you've typed, matching the selected language and formatting!
